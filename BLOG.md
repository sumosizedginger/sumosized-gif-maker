# SumoSized: A Manifesto for the Independent Creator 🎨🔥

## To the Artists, Authors, and Digital Renegades

When I built **SumoSized GIF Maker**, I didn't build it for corporate boardrooms. I built it for **you**.

In an era where every "free" tool is a data-harvesting machine and every digital asset is scraped into a black-box model, I believe your creative process deserves a sanctuary. Your art, your clips, and your source files are yours—and they should stay that way.

### Why SumoSized is Your New Creative Arsenal

#### 1. Zero Transmission, Total Sovereignty 🛡️

Your videos never hit my servers because I don't **have** servers for your media. Everything is processed in your browser’s memory using a local FFmpeg.wasm engine.

- **For Authors**: Keep your book trailers and reveal clips secure before they hit the public.
- **For Artists**: Process your time-lapses without worrying about third-party "rights" to your WIP (Work In Progress).

#### 2. Elite Quality Controls (No "Average" Results) 💎

Most browser GIF makers give you grainy, optimized-for-size junk. I give you the "Advanced Quality" arsenal:

- **Dithering Control**: Choose the smooth gradients of _Floyd-Steinberg_ or the retro, textured vibe of _Bayer_.
- **Color Depth**: A granular 2-256 color slider to hit that perfect balance between filing size and visual fidelity.
- **Frame Diffing**: Use "Rectangle Mode" to slash file sizes by up to 60% without losing a single pixel of quality.

#### 3. Professional Geometric Warp 📐

Need to skew a clip to fit perfectly inside a digital mockup or a retro monitor? My **8-point Perspective Warp** (Corner Pinning) gives you the spatial precision of professional video suites like After Effects, right in your browser.

#### 4. The Filter Studio 🎞️

Layer up to 10 cinematic filters simultaneously. Drag and drop them to change the render order. Save your entire setup as a **Project Preset** (.json) so you can return to your "signature look" for every project.

### Community Driven

**SumoSized** is Open Source (MIT). It’s a tool built by creators, for creators. No subscriptions. No watermarks. No data selling. Just absolute creative power.

**Go create something legendary.**
